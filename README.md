# World Pays — Field Notes

Two interactive slides accompanying the LinkedIn essay *Who Tells the Stories. Who Moves the Money.*

A payments practitioner's field notes on global financial inclusion, mapped against UN Sustainable Development Goals and World Bank development indices.

**Last Update:** May 2026

## Why this exists

I have spent fifteen years inside the payments industry. The companion essay traces what happened when I noticed that the digital under-presence of non-dominant cultures and the financial under-presence of the Global South share the same structural shape — and what the cultural sphere might have to teach payments about custodianship.

Not a pitch. A thought experiment.
